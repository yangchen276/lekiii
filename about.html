the Mother Daughter Digital Program is a learning helps mothers and daughters develop digital skills together while strengthening their relationship through shared activities and team work.The program teaches basic computer skills, internet safety, digital creativity, and communication tools useful for education, business, and daily life. Through fun activities such as digital storytelling, online games.virtual cooking, and digital art, participants build confidence in technology, improve communication, and prepare for future opportunities in the digital world. 🤝 Learning Together One special feature of the program is that mothers and daughters learn side by side. They support each other, complete digital activities together, and build stronger relationships through teamwork. This shared learning experience makes education: More enjoyable More meaningful More practical and interactive.

Purpose of the Program: The main purpose of this program is to Improve digital literacy for mothers and daughters Build confidence in using modern technology Encourage teamwork and communication within families Prepare participants for future educational and career opportunities Promote safe and responsible use of the internet
